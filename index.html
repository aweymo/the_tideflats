An audio project about distance, forgetting and the history of the Tacoma Tideflats.
Created by Andrew Weymouth for the University of Washington's Storytelling Fellows.


Thank you to the band Old Saw, composer Henry Birdsey and label Lobby Art Records for use of the album Country Tropics in this work.


Thanks also to Anna Trammell and Spencer Bowman of the Tacoma Public Library for helping to inform and shape this work.



This work is an extension of the Lost Tacoma Project,
where I had first studied blueprints for the Carstens Packing facility and the Tideflats area.
